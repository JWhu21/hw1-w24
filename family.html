Family

Our Midwest Adventure

In 2016, I loaded my kids into the minivan and took them on a drive across the Northern Midwest states. We visited Wisconsin Dells, the Badlands, Mount Rushmore, Jewel Cave, and Custer State Park.

Unexpected Challenges

I didn't plan for the fact that the Sturgis Motorcycle rally would overlap with our trip. Many towns were closed to through traffic, and the lack of internet required that we use paper maps often.
